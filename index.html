Who Are We?

We are a team of dedicated tutors and teachers of Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. We offer tutoring, test prep, and fun projects taught by the most studious of students and teachers with years of experience... for FREE. At TJ Lift, our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators.

"TJHSST LIFT has changed the lives of hundres of aspiring thinkers. I say with pride that we, as a community are making changed"

Jay Wickliff, Coordinator
